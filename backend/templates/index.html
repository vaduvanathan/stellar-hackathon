Set up nominee

Your funds stay in your Stellar account — nothing is locked in a contract. Create a secondary (sweep) key, add it as a co-signer, and register a nominee.

After no activity for the set period, we send an SMS with a claim link. Your nominee answers a secret question and can sweep the full balance to their address.

How to get a Stellar account

  1. Open Stellar Laboratory – Account Creator (Test network).
  2. Click Generate keypair. Copy your Public key (G…) and Secret key (S…). Keep the secret safe.
  3. Paste your public key and click Fund account to get test XLM from Friendbot.
  4. Use the Public key (G…) as your account when you create a secondary key and register a nominee above.

Or fund via URL: https://friendbot.stellar.org/?addr=YOUR_PUBLIC_KEY
